Website Evaluation - Reddit - Audience

Who is the site for? Who are the intended users?

Users looking for the latest news deemed noteworthy by the reddit community. A majority of the community has a technical background or is tech savvy.

What do users want to accomplish?

Users want to read the latest and top news as well as participate in discussion with other users around topics of their interest.

What are the needed skills? Good fit with skills of intended users?

Users need to understand site dynamics. The reddiquette page helps describe how the website ranks articles and the proper reasons to upvote or downvote articles.

Users also may want to search for articles with small bits of information or read news relating to only one subject.

The search and subreddit filters help provide this functionality.

The audience is generally tech savvy enough to understand how the website works, understand search, subreddits, and read the redditquette

What tasks are users trying to accomplish?

Users would like to familiarize themselves with news centered around a specific interest. Users' can subscribe to subreddits, small communities that make posts relating to one specific interest.

Users would like to read the top news relating too all of their aggregate interests. If logged in, the home page will show users this data sorted by the reddit ranking system.

In addition, users would like to discuss articles posted on reddit. The forum style comment threading allows for detailed threaded discussion within article pages.

Finally, users would like to search for articles with specific keywords or find old articles that have since disappeared from the home page
