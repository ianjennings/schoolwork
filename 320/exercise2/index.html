Website Evaluation - Reddit - Summary

Reddit is a social news aggregation site. News articles are submitted by users into a stream of submissions. Each submission has a karma value. Every user can either add one point of karma to an article or remove one point. Articles with the most points for a given amount of time end up on the home page. Each article also has a discussion page. Here, users can comment on articles. Each comment has a similar karma system, with top scoring comments appearing above lower scoring comments.

Articles can be submitted to subreddits; sub categories for articles of a specific interest.

The overall karma scores of comments and submissions by each user is kept in their profile. Having more karma does not make your vote more powerful.

The site has a bland look with a large focus on text. There is a very limited use of icons. Icons that are used are very small, simple, and require little explanation.

There is a simple search feature that appears on the top right of every page on the website.
