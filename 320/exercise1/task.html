Website Evaluation - Reddit - Task

Support tasks users need to accomplish?

Users want to search for a specific article or browse current news. News articles can be sorted by a variety of functions. The search bar is located at the top of the page and follows the general conventions for search.

Support both experienced and inexperienced users?

Inexperienced users (although turned off by the sites bland color scheme and layout) can navigate and operate the website. They can browse news and search. However, experienced users may be disappointed by reddits limited search functionality.

User expectations meet?

Yes, users can browse news relating to specific categories and interests. Once an article is located, users can participate in a threaded discussion about the article.
